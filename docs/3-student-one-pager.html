STUDENT GUIDE · MATRA ASSESSMENT PLATFORM

How to take your exam

Everything is at exams.matramedia.co.in

Before you start, have these three things ready:
  • Your exam code — 8 characters, shared by your college (notice board / admit card / instructor).
  • Your roll number — as registered by your college.
  • Your email — the one your college has on record for you.

Three simple steps

1

Check your device

A day before, on the device you'll use, open /check. It tests your connection, browser, and that the text displays correctly — 30 seconds.

2

Find your exam

On exam day, go to /exam, enter your exam code, then your roll number and email. Your exam appears.

3

Take it

Read the instructions, then begin. The exam starts and ends automatically at the scheduled time.

During the exam

  • Questions appear one at a time; you cannot go back.
  • The timer is shown at the top — the exam submits automatically when it ends.
  • Stay in fullscreen and don't switch tabs — both are logged for your invigilator.
  • Your answers save as you go.

If your internet drops

  • Don't panic — your saved answers are safe.
  • A banner tells you answers are waiting to sync; they send automatically when you reconnect.
  • If the page closes, just reopen the exam link and continue from where you were.
  • Only one device/tab at a time — close any extra copies.
Wrong code or "details don't match"? Re-check the exam code, and that your roll number and email are exactly as your college registered them. If it still won't work, contact your instructor.

After the exam

  • You'll see a confirmation, and your score if your college has enabled results.
  • If your exam is part of a multi-module series, you'll see your running total so far.
  • Certificates (if issued) carry a code and QR — anyone can confirm a certificate is genuine at /verify.

Tip: use a charged device on a stable connection, in a quiet place, and arrive a few minutes early.
